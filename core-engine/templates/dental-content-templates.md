3. If possible, reinsert into socket

1. Save any broken pieces
2. Rinse mouth with warm water

**How long do I need to wear Invisalign each day?**
20-22 hours per day. Only remove for eating, drinking (except water), brushing, and flossing.

**Does Invisalign hurt?**

**Can I eat with Invisalign?**
No, always remove aligners before eating or drinking anything except water.

**How do I clean my aligners?**
Rinse with lukewarm water and brush gently. Use Invisalign cleaning crystals or Retainer Brite weekly.

**Will Invisalign affect my speech?**
Most patients adjust within a few days. You may have a slight lisp initially.

**What happens after Invisalign treatment?**
